About Us

Today’s Life Foundation is an NGO that helps in the eradication of hunger and poverty in Nigeria. It was established in the year 2010 and was registered under CAC in the year 2017. The founder of the organization Alhaji Abdulaziz was a popular politycian from Northern Nigeria and he is well known for helping the less privileged and and putting a smile on their faces in the society. She has helped over 100 families in the Northern part of Nigeria for paying their children medical bills and more than 500 students are under the foundation benefiting from it through the payments of their school fees and empowering their parents in different fields!
